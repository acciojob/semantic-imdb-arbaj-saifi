Summary

*Inception* is a sci-fi thriller directed by Christopher Nolan, where a skilled thief, Dom Cobb, is given the ultimate task: implanting an idea into a target’s subconscious. The film explores the boundaries of dreams and reality through a layered narrative that keeps audiences questioning what is real.

Actors

The movie stars Leonardo DiCaprio as Dom Cobb, with supporting roles by Joseph Gordon-Levitt, Ellen Page, Tom Hardy, Ken Watanabe, Cillian Murphy, and Marion Cotillard.
